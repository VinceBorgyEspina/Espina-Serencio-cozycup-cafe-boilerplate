Discover the story behind CozyCup Café...

CozyCup Café is a passion project of two friends who both used the power of coffee to get through their college lives. It is dedicated to providing warmth to its patrons either by giving them a safe place to pass time or a warm beverage to kick the day off.

------------------------------------------

Meet the Team

Vince Borgy Espina

Co-founder, a coffee connoisseur who has a soft spot for cozy places and a a great fascination for quality pastry.

------------------------------------------

Jason Maverick Serencio

Co-founder, a young lad who grew up around the food industry and puts value for quality products and top of the line service.

------------------------------------------

Café's Mission

WHY CozyCup EXISTS: CozyCup is a place to sleep, play games, read, listen to music, chat with friends or just enjoy the vibe, drinks, and pastries. It aims to provide a safe space for casual coffee drinkers and coffee enthusiasts alike.
